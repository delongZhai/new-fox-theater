About Us

New Fox Theatre


New Fox Theatre was founded in 1984 by Jaime Fox for the small town of Stowe, Vermont. It is the home of the town's most entertaining performances, ranging from ballets to plays. The theatre offers a type of entertainment suited for everyone, in all age groups. So grab a ticket and head down today!
